About School

name:

Aptakisic Junior High School

Aptakisic Junior High School is a highly rated, public school located in Buffalo Grove, IL. It has 755 students in grades 6-8 with a student-teacher ratio of 14 to 1. According to state test scores, 71% of students are at least proficient in math and 75% in reading. I love Aptakisic! It’s such a nice, environmental friendly school. The staff there are so kind and very respectful. I am a student there and I am telling you that they don’t stress us or anything, but they are very strict about discipline, which if you think about it, it’s a good thing! We have all the resources that we’ll need and overall I just love it! Its a great place to make friends. Most of the teachers are very flexible. Everyone at Aptakisic is very respectfull and kind. The percentage of students achieving proficiency in math is 71% (which is higher than the Illinois state average of 32%) for the 2017-18 school year. The percentage of students achieving proficiency in reading/language arts is 75% (which is higher than the Illinois state average of 37%) for the 2017-18 school year. about school district

The Aptakisic-Tripp School District is a school district in Lake County, Illinois. It operates four schools and covers grades Early Childhood through 8. Most students live in Buffalo Grove, a northern suburb of Chicago, and some live in unincorporated Prairie View or Deerfield. District 102 covers seven square miles in Lake County, IL. We serve a diverse population of approximately 2,590 students representing over 61 different cultures and languages. Our students live in Buffalo Grove, Prairie View, and unincorporated sections of Deerfield.
